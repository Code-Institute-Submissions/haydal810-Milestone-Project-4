About Us

The Authentic Irish Sweater Co. was setup in 2010, in Glencar, Co. Kerry by local businessman Seamus O'Toole. His Jumpers & Sweaters are of a quintessential Irish design that perfectly compliments a smart or casual outfit. Seamus makes all his own clothing products, in the heart of the kingdom of Kerry, so you can be assured that you are buying a guarenteed Irish Product.
